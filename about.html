For the Youth of the Community

HSLQ Challenges

The youth of Huntington Station face significant challenges in education, access to resources, and community support. Many families struggle with limited opportunities for enrichment and mentorship. HSLQ works to bridge these gaps by creating programs and events that empower young people to reach their full potential.

The Solution

Is Up To Us

Change begins at the local level. By bringing together residents, business owners, educators, and community leaders, HSLQ creates a united front that addresses the real needs of our neighborhood. Together we can build programs, improve our streets, and create opportunities that lift up every member of our community.

Community Morale

Role Models

Strong communities are built by strong role models. HSLQ is committed to highlighting and supporting the individuals and organizations that set a positive example for our youth. From local business leaders to dedicated volunteers, these role models inspire the next generation to dream big and give back.
