Why HSLQ Formed?

The Friends of the Huntington Station Latin Quarter formed to address the needs of one of Long Island's most vibrant and diverse communities. We believe that by working together, residents and businesses can create positive change and build a stronger neighborhood for everyone.

Our Name

Why Call it a Latin Quarter?

A "Latin Quarter" is a designation used around the world to identify neighborhoods that are culturally vibrant, rich in history, and serve as hubs for art, cuisine, and community life. By embracing this identity, Huntington Station celebrates its diverse heritage and the energy that makes the area special.
