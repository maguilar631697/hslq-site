Holiday 2025

2025 Toy Drive

Adopt a family this holiday season! With a $75 minimum per child, you can help make the holidays brighter for families in need. We currently have 180+ children on our list waiting for generous donors.

Gifts can be dropped off at the Palacios Law Group / HSLQ Headquarters at 1399 New York Avenue, Huntington Station. Contact Laura Palacios to adopt a family today.

Annual Toy Drive

Once Friends of HSLQ gathers information, a list is created which is distributed to Friends and members who in turn purchase gifts for these deserving families.

Over the years, Friends of HSLQ has collected hundreds of gifts which have been donated to hundreds of families.

2025 Annual Neighborhood Cleanup

Volunteers rolled up their sleeves and got to work at the 15th Annual Huntington Station Clean Up! Year after year, our community comes together to beautify the streets and show pride in where we live.

This event is a powerful demonstration of what happens when neighbors unite for a common cause.

Community Meals

HSLQ regularly organizes community meal events, bringing neighbors together over food and conversation. These gatherings strengthen bonds and ensure no one in our community goes hungry.
