2025 Event

2025 Annual Toy Drive

HSLQ is proud to host our annual Toy Drive for the families of Huntington Station. Each year we adopt families in need and provide holiday gifts for their children. Donations of new, unwrapped toys can be dropped off at designated locations throughout the community.

If you would like to adopt a family, volunteer, or make a donation, please contact us. Together we can make the holidays brighter for our neighbors.

Annual Toy Drive
